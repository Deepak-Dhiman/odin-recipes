Tasty maggi

Delicious 5-minute maggi noodles to relish hunger when you are short of time or just in the mood to eat something nice.

Ingredients:

  • Maggi packet.
  • Chopped onions.
  • Chopped tomatoes.
  • Salt and pepper.

Steps:

  1. Heat up 1 Â½ cups of water in an open pan to just boiling level.
  2. Add maggi noodles and maggi masala into it.
  3. Add salt and pepper for added taste.
  4. Add chopped onions and tomatoes while stirring.
  5. Keep stirring to cook evenly until only a small amount of water is remaining.
  6. Serve hot.
